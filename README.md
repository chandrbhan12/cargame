# Turbo Velocity 3D 🏎️✨

A premium 3D racing game built with **Three.js** and **Vite**. Experience high-speed racing with dynamic environments, multiple car models, and realistic synthesized sounds.

## Features 🚀
- **Dynamic Environments**: Cycle through City, Desert, Night, and Cyberpunk themes every 1000m.
- **Car Selection**: Choose from 5 iconic models including BMW M4, Audi R8, Thar 4x4, Classic Sedan, and Hyper X.
- **Advanced Audio**: Realistic engine synthesis that reacts to speed, plus coin and crash sound effects.
- **Mobile Responsive**: Full touch controls and optimized HUD for mobile devices.
- **Smooth Gameplay**: 60 FPS rendering with infinite road recycling and procedural traffic generation.

## Controls 🎮
### PC (Keyboard)
- **Arrow Keys / WASD**: Steering & Speed control.
- **Space**: Start Engine.

### Mobile (Touch)
- **On-screen Arrows**: Steer, Accelerate, and Brake.

## Tech Stack 🛠️
- **Engine**: Three.js
- **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript
- **Styling**: Modern CSS3 (Glassmorphism & Neon Aesthetics)
- **Bundler**: Vite

## How to Play 🏁
1. Pick your car on the start screen.
2. Toggle sound on/off as preferred.
3. Click **START ENGINE**.
4. Dodge traffic, collect coins, and reach the highest distance possible!
